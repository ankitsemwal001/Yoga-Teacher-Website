❖ What is Hatha Yoga?

Hatha yoga is a foundational practice that focuses on physical postures (asanas) and breathing techniques (pranayama). The word "Hatha" represents the balance between the sun (ha) and the moon (tha).

In our classes, we move at a deliberate, mindful pace to ensure proper form and deep connection. It is designed to align and purify the body, preparing the mind for deeper meditation.
